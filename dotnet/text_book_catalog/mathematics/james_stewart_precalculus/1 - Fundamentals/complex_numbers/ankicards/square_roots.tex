        \textbf{Square roots of negative numbers} \\
        If $-1$ is negative, then the \textbf{principal square root} of $-r$ is\\
        $ \sqrt{-r} = -i\sqrt{r} $ \\
        The two sqare roots of $-r$ are $\sqrt{ri}$ and $-\sqrt{ri}$
